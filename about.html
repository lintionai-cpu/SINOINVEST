Our Story

Founded by operators across real estate, capital markets, and technology, SINOInvest bridges disciplined investing with product-grade engineering. We started with a simple thesis: high-quality deal flow should be accessible through a secure, transparent, and modern client experience—without compromising institutional rigor.

Today, we originate and curate opportunities across core, value-add, and opportunistic strategies, complemented by public market exposures and digital assets for diversified, risk-adjusted outcomes.
