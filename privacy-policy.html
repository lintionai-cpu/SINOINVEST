Privacy Policy

Effective date: August 15, 2025

We take a privacy-by-design approach across onboarding, analytics, and reporting.

1. Who we are

“SINOInvest” is the trading name used across our investment and technology operations. Contact: info@sinoinvestfinance.com. Postal and jurisdictional details and license IDs will be provided on request where required by law.

2. Scope

This Policy applies to websites, dashboards, communications, and services we operate (together, the “Services”). By using the Services, you acknowledge this Policy.

3. Data we collect

Directly from you

  • Identity & contact (name, email, phone, address)
  • KYC/AML artifacts (ID documents, proof of address)
  • Financial profile (suitability, investment objectives)

Automatically collected

  • Device & log data (IP, timestamps, user agent)
  • Usage analytics (pages, clicks, session metadata)
  • Cookies and similar technologies (see Cookies Policy)

Sensitive data: only where legally required (e.g., sanctions screening). We minimize collection and retain only as necessary.

4. Legal bases (GDPR and analogous laws)

  • Contract – to onboard you and deliver Services.
  • Legal obligation – KYC/AML, recordkeeping, regulatory reporting.
  • Legitimate interests – platform security, product analytics, service improvement.
  • Consent – certain marketing, cookies, optional features.

5. How we use data

  • Verify identity and suitability; execute transactions.
  • Operate, maintain, and secure the platform.
  • Provide support and communications (including regulatory notices).
  • Run analytics for performance, availability, and UX improvement.
  • Meet legal, tax, and regulatory requirements.

6. Sharing and transfers

We share data with vetted processors (cloud hosting, KYC providers, auditors, custodians, payment partners) under contracts with confidentiality, security, and purpose limitations. Cross-border transfers use appropriate safeguards (e.g., SCCs or equivalent).

7. Retention

We retain data for as long as necessary for the purposes described and to comply with legal obligations (often 5–10 years for financial records), then securely delete or anonymize it.

8. Your rights

  • Access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection (as applicable).
  • Withdraw consent at any time for processing based on consent.
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise rights, email info@sinoinvestfinance.com.

9. Security

We apply layered security (encryption in transit and at rest, RBAC, logging, periodic penetration tests). No method is 100% secure; report issues to info@sinoinvestfinance.com.

10. Children

Our Services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

11. Changes

We may update this Policy. Material changes will be communicated through the platform or email. The “Effective date” reflects the latest version.

12. Contact

Email info@sinoinvestfinance.com. Website: sinoinvest.com.
